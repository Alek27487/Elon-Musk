Companies


SpaceX (2002): Founded to reduce the cost of space travel and make human life multiplanetary. SpaceX has achieved numerous milestones, including the development of reusable rockets and the Dragon spacecraft. The company's Starship project aims to enable missions to Mars.

Tesla, Inc. (2004): Musk joined Tesla a year after its founding and became the CEO and product architect. Tesla is known for its electric vehicles, energy storage solutions, and commitment to sustainable energy. Under Musk's leadership, Tesla became the world's most valuable carmaker by market capitalization.

Twitter/X (2022): Musk acquired Twitter in 2022 and rebranded it as "X," making various changes to the platform's functionality and business model.

PayPal (1999): Co-founded X.com, an online payment service, which later became PayPal and was acquired by eBay in 2002.

The Boring Company (2016): Focuses on reducing urban traffic through underground tunnels.
